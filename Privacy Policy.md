# Privacy Policy

By inviting Challenge Buddy to your Discord server, you confirm that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

While using Challenge Buddy, you must also comply with Discord’s:
- [Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms)
- [Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)


## Data Collected

Challenge Buddy collects limited data only when necessary to provide its services.

The following data may be stored:
- Discord User IDs — stored when a user purchases a Challenge Buddy subscription in order to apply and manage subscription-related perks.
Challenge Buddy does not collect message content, usernames, email addresses, or any other personal data.

Collected data is used solely for the following purposes:
- Managing and verifying active subscriptions
- Granting and maintaining subscription perks


## Payments

All payments and subscription transactions are handled entirely by Discord. Challenge Buddy does not collect, process, or store any payment information, billing details, or financial data.


## Data Storage & Retention

Subscription-related data is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to provide the associated perks. If a subscription expires or is canceled, the associated data may be removed within a reasonable time frame.


## Data Sharing

Challenge Buddy does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Data is only shared where required to operate through Discord’s API or to comply with legal obligations.


## Children’s Privacy

Challenge Buddy does not knowingly collect or store data from users under the age of 13.


## Your Rights

You may request:
- Access to the data associated with your user ID
- Deletion of your data, where applicable
Removing Challenge Buddy from a server will stop any further data collection.


## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.  
Continued use of Challenge Buddy after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.


## Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding privacy or data handling, you may join the official [support server](https://discord.gg/NeVUGREx9S).

This Privacy Policy can also be accessed using the `/botinfo` command within Discord.
